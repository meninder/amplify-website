I was going to write an article about how resilience can be a bad thing, but found this article by HBR captures my sentiment. In summary:

"...too much resilience could make people overly tolerant of adversity. At work, this can translate into putting up with boring or demoralizing jobs — and particularly bad bosses — for longer than needed."
